# TruthGuard-X (TGX 2.0) - Project Definition & Features

## What is TruthGuard-X?
TruthGuard-X is an autonomous, premium AI-powered misinformation detection system explicitly designed for the Indian digital ecosystem. It acts as an advanced "digital forensic lab," helping users investigate news articles, text snippets, URLs, and images to determine whether they are factual, misleading, or entirely fabricated (Fake News).

The platform is built with a **"Vedic-Futurism"** aesthetic—blending ancient authority and academic gravitas with cutting-edge cybersecurity and artificial intelligence UI design.

---

## Core Features & Capabilities

### 1. The Analysis Hub (Core Intelligence Engine)
The central investigative tool where users can cross-examine suspicious content.
*   **Text & Snippet Verification:** Users paste claims or news snippets, and the AI cross-references them against trusted databases to expose biases, logical fallacies, or falsehoods.
*   **Source URL Scanning:** Users input a website link, and the system scans the source node to track credibility and potential network tracing.
*   **Zero-Knowledge Protocol UI:** While scanning, the interface provides real-time terminal-like feedback (e.g., "Processing logic stream...") to ensure users understand complex verifications are taking place.

### 2. Deepfake Scanner (Image/Video Forensic Matrix)
A specialized tool designed to combat highly fabricated synthetic media.
*   **Visual Ingestion:** Users upload images (and potentially videos) to scan for AI-generated artifacts.
*   **Synthetic Probability Scoring:** Evaluates media pixel-by-pixel for manipulation, deepfake generation, or hidden metadata.

### 3. "Bharat Edition" Multilingual Switcher
Misinformation in India spreads heavily through regional languages. TruthGuard-X is built for true accessibility across regions.
*   **Supported Languages:** English, Hindi (हिन्दी), Punjabi (ਪੰਜਾਬੀ), Marathi (मराठी), Bengali (বাংলা), and Tamil (தமிழ்).
*   **Live Translation:** The UI seamlessly adapts to the chosen dialect to ensure localized, accurate context. 

### 4. AI-Assist Chatbot (Llama-3.3-70B Powered)
A conversational agent integrated directly into the dashboard.
*   **Incident & Digital Hygiene Expert:** Users can ask the bot how to spot phishing, report a cybercrime, or verify a specific forward on WhatsApp. 
*   **Contextual Awareness:** The chatbot can keep a history of the current reporting session.

### 5. Legal-Grade FIR Report Generator
*   **Forensic Documentation:** Once misinformation is verified, the system generates a formal, high-authority document (resembling official Government/Legal documentation).
*   **Evidence Logging:** It compiles the user's initial evidence alongside the AI's cryptographic verification to be submitted to law enforcement or fact-checking agencies.

### 6. Transparency Dashboard & Node Metrics
Users have a "bird's-eye view" of global threat levels.
*   **Accuracy Ratings & Threat Radar:** Displays system confidence levels (e.g., >94% Accuracy).
*   **Credibility Gauge:** Results are returned in a visually accessible "Truth-O-Meter" (Green = Reliable, Amber = Misleading, Red = Fake News).

## Target Audience
*   **General Consumers:** People looking to verify WhatsApp forwards and social media claims.
*   **Journalists & Fact-Checkers:** Professionals requiring a fast, highly accurate first-pass verification tool.
*   **Law Enforcement & Civic Bodies:** Authorities needing standardized, verifiable documentation (FIR Reports) of digital misinformation campaigns.
